# tofuvideo — brand kit

The single source of truth for tofuvideo's visual identity: the marketing site, the VideoEngine
dashboard, and anything else that carries the brand. Every value here is final — copy tokens from
this file, never eyeball them.

The mascot: a Siamese cat hugging a tofu block. The brand in one line: **soft on effort, sharp on
results** — warm and a little playful on the surface, precise underneath.

**Rules**

- The sticker keeps its white outline — always. Place it on cream (`#FDF9F0`–`#FAF3E3`), on
  brown-950, or on coral; the outline guarantees separation on all three.
- Clear space: half the tofu block's width on every side. Minimum size: 32 px tall (below that,
  use the cat-head icon).
- Wordmark-in-text lockup: **tofu** in brown-900 + **video** in coral-500, set in Baloo 2 Bold,
  lowercase, no space.
- Don't recolor, outline, rotate past ±8°, add shadows other than a soft drop, or place the full
  sticker on busy imagery.

## 5. Accessibility (measured, WCAG 2.1)

Key ratios — full matrix regenerable via `scripts` in the site scratchpad history:

- brown-900 on cream-50 **12.98** (AAA) · brown-700 on cream-50 **6.78** (AA) · brown-500 on
  cream-50 **3.44** (large only)
- **White on coral-500 is 2.73 — a known miss.** Rules: white text needs coral-600+ (3.40, bold
  14 px+ / large) or coral-700 (4.47, any size); on coral-500 use brown-950 text (5.86) or
  reserve it for non-text fills. Dashboard primary buttons are therefore **coral-600**.
- Dark UI: mink-200 on mink-950 **10.95**, mink-400 on mink-950 **6.35**, and every accent-300
  (coral/amber/tofublue/matcha/heather/berry) is **7+** (AAA) on mink-950.
- Badges: text-700 on hue-100 passes 3:1+ for every hue; berry and matcha pass 4.5.

## 6. Typography

| Role | Face | Weights | Rules |
|---|---|---|---|
| Display / headings / buttons | **Baloo 2** | 600–800 | Lowercase-friendly, tight leading (1.05–1.2). Never below 14 px |
| Body / UI | **Inter** | 400, 600 | 15–18 px body, 1.5–1.65 leading |
| Numbers in UI | Inter + `tabular-nums` | | timelines, stats, durations |

Type scale (marketing): 64/48 display · 40 h2 · 24 h3 · 18–20 lede · 16 body · 13–14 small.
Dashboard: 20 page title · 14 body · 12 secondary · 10–11 uppercase labels (+0.05 em tracking).

## 7. Shape & elevation

- Radius: cards 24 px (`rounded-3xl`), inputs/tiles 12 px, buttons & pills full.
- Shadows (warm — brown-tinted, never pure black):
  `soft: 0 2px 8px rgb(59 42 33 / .06), 0 12px 32px rgb(59 42 33 / .08)`
  `lift: 0 4px 12px rgb(59 42 33 / .08), 0 24px 48px rgb(59 42 33 / .12)`
- Icons: 24 px grid, 1.8 stroke, round caps/joins (lucide style).
- Motion: 150–300 ms ease; a single playful rotate (±1–6°) is on-brand, more is noise.

## 8. Voice

Warm, confident, a little playful. Short sentences, concrete benefits, at most one gentle
tofu/cat pun per surface. Buttons say what happens ("Start for free", "Regenerate image").
Errors say what broke and the fix. Think Notion/Linear copy with a mascot.

## 9. Re-theming the VideoEngine dashboard

The dashboard (`videoengine/frontend`) is Tailwind v4, dark-only, with **no token layer** —
~622 hardcoded default-palette utilities across 29 files (zinc 419, indigo 91, red 39, amber 36,
emerald 26, violet 7, fuchsia 6, sky 5). Do **not** find-and-replace. Tailwind v4 lets you
redefine the default palette variables, so every existing class remaps globally.

**Step 1** — paste [`brand/videoengine-theme.css`](brand/videoengine-theme.css) into
`frontend/app/globals.css` right after `@import "tailwindcss";`. It remaps:

| Engine classes | Become | Why |
|---|---|---|
| `indigo-*` | coral (mid-ramp shifted one step dark) | primary; keeps white button labels ≥3:1 |
| `zinc-*` | mink | warm neutrals instead of cold gray |
| `emerald-*` | matcha | success/voice |
| `amber-*` | brand amber | warning/in-progress |
| `red-*` | berry | error |
| `sky-*` | tofublue | 📷 stock badge/track |
| `violet-*`, `fuchsia-*` | heather | review + ✨ AI (one "magic" family, intentionally) |

**Step 2** — the block also replaces the `::selection` indigo in `globals.css:8`.

**Step 3** — three raw-hex touchpoints, by hand:
- `#F07E68` → `#EF7A70` in `components/Logo.tsx:39`, `components/Sidebar.tsx:98`,
  `components/LoginScreen.tsx:40`
- Logo palette in `Logo.tsx`: face `#F7EBD0`→`#FAF3E3`, outline `#4A3226`→`#3B2A21`,
  cheeks `#F3A48F`→`#F4948B` (or drop the drawn logo for `logo.png`)

**Step 4 (optional, changes rendered videos)** — default caption highlight in
`lib/api.ts:323-328`: `#22DD88` → `#EF7A70` so burned captions are on-brand out of the box.

Stock/AI badge colors are duplicated in `SceneCard.tsx` and `TimelineEditor.tsx` — the remap
covers both, but if you later hand-edit one, edit the other.
